About GitFunded

GitFunded is an intuitive and easy-to-use crowdfunding app that empowers users to create, personalize, and manage charitable campaigns. With GitFunded, users can explore and support a wide range of charitable causes that align with their values and make a real impact in their communities and beyond. The app makes it simple to donate and monitor progress, while also providing valuable insights and tools to help campaign organizers maximize their fundraising potential. Whether you're a charitable organization or a compassionate individual looking to make a difference, GitFunded is the perfect platform to help bring your cause to life.
